Our Menu

Ribeye

This American Wagyu Prime Ribeye Tomahawk from Snake River Farms will have a beautiful marbling of fat, delivering unparalelled flavor and exceptional texture. It is finished with a housemade steak butter with Kobe Beef talow.

Scallops

Our Scallops are caught and brought in fresh every day, and hand opened from the shell to order. They are seared to perfection, leaving a mouth watering tenderness with an amazing flavor profile.

Chicken

Our Chicken is brought in from a local farmer, who raises pasture raised chickens, feeding them organically. They are deliciously juicy and tender, and served with our housemade parmesan mushroom risotto.
